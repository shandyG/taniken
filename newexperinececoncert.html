New Experience Concert

Kentaro Taninaka, Kazunori Takashio, Satoko Takita, Tomoya Asai

The effects of COVID-19 have brought about various changes to the entertainment we experience. Especially in environments that does not allow close conversation, the creation of new environments using technology was required. Focusing on the classical music concert, we have implemented a virtual live-viewing space that can move real and virtual concert spaces. By using telepresence robots and extended-reality technologies, we provided the new live-experiences for both audiences and performers.
