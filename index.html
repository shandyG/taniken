ABOUT

I am a 4th-year PhD student in the Graduate School of Media and Governance at Keio University, where I am very grateful to be advised by Prof. Kazunori Takashio.

I create systems that uses AR to augment the functions of everyday objects and improve people's AR experiences.

My research uses AR and VR technologies to implement systems for local residents living in Tottori and other prefectures, as well as for students on campus.
